Welcome to MyEye Camp, eye therapy software!

This software helps your eyes improve their ability to move and their endurance.

To get started, construct a routine by pressing buttons with the "+" symbol on them.

Or, use one of our preset routines.

Then, select the go button to play the routine.

Click the about and info for more information on how to operate this website.

Pro Tips!

Each exercise has a default of 6 reps. Doubling up exercises gives you longer, 12 rep exercises.

It is recommended to keep a routine under six exercises. Two or three routines a day is good!

Do different types of exercises each day. Tracking and Convergence go well together. Saccades works well with other Saccades exercises.

Do exercises at least four times a week. This way, you'll see rapid improvements.

It is recommended to start with the preset "starter set" exercises. This way, you'll get a feel for making routines and advance to Intermediate and Advanced exercises quickly.
